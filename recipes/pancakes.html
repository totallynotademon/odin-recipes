Fluffy Pancakes

You don't need a box to make great fluffy pancakes. Making them from scratch is just as easy and so much better.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup buttermilk *
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg>
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • cooking spray
  • Maple syrup

* If you don't have buttermilk, add two tablespoons of white vinegar to 3/4 cup regular milk and let sit for 5 minutes

Directions:

  1. Place flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt into a mixing bowl and whisk to combine
  2. Place milk, butter, and egg into a separate mixing bowl and whisk to combine
  3. Pour dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and whisk until all lumps are gone. Set aside.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Coat with cooking spray
  5. Pour desired amount of batter onto skillet. This recipe works best making 2-3 smaller pancakes rather than 1 large pancake.
  6. Cook pancakes until small bubbles start to form on the surface. Flip pancake and continue to cook until brown on the other side.
  7. Serve warm with maple syrup.
