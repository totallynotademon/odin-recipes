Peanut Butter Cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg

Directions:

  1. Heat oven to 325 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Mix peanut butter, sugar, and egg with a large spoon until well blended
  3. Roll dough into 24 balls. Place 4 inches apart on a baking sheet. Flatten balls with a fork.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes, or until lightly browned. Do not overbake. Cool for 5 minutes on baking sheet, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
  5. Try not to eat all of them in one sitting.
