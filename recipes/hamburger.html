Hamburgers

Ingredients

  • 1-lb medium ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Sliced cheese of your choice (optional)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Choice of toppings (Lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, ketchup, mustard, mayonaisse)

Directions

  1. Let ground beef come to room temperature before forming into 4 - 1/4 lb patties.
  2. Prepare toppings. Thin slices of pickles, tomatoes, and onions work the best. Lettuce can be whole or shredded.
  3. Pre-heat cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Generously season patties with salt and pepper before placing in skillet.
  5. Cook patties for 3-5 minutes or until browned, then flip.
  6. After flipping, place a slice of cheese on each patty. Pour a small amount of water into the skillet and cover with a lid. This allows the cheese to melt. Cook for another 3-5 minutes.
  7. Place burger on bun and top with desired toppings.
